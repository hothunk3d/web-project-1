Donlaw4cs Resources, Port Harcourt

Donlaw4c's Resources was incorporated in Nigeria in December, 2021 as a wholly indigenous service Company based in Port Harcourt, Rivers State. The registered office is located at No. 1 Okoloma Town Hall Road Okoloma Afam, Oyigbo, Port Harcourt, Rivers State.

We are actively engaged in providing quality Engineering and consultancy services with good innovative technical solutions to the Energy industries (which includes Power, Oil and Gas) in Nigeria. A highlight of few of our engagement in the oil and gas industries includes but not limited to: engagement in the business of Oil and Gas, Oilfield services, Energy services, supplies of marketable oil products to filling stations, Oil tools and distribution/sale of oil tools equipment for use in the Oil and Gas sector

As an integrated consortium, DONLAW4C'S RESOURCES has wide range of technological capability in petroleum and related services coupled with adequate plant and equipment to ensure accuracy, quality and rapid growth.

OUR MISSION

We continuously strive to be Africa's leading Oil and Gas Company, with unparalleled commitment to partnership and reputation for quality products and services. Ensuring operational Health and Safety of our environment for both our employees, clients and visitors is our sole aim to achieving a safe/hazard free working environment that will not only protect the personnel on the site but also the interest of the company which aims at always making certain that people are always safe from danger in all spheres of work.
